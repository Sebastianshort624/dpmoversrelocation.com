FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my move?
−
For local moves, 2–3 weeks is ideal. For long-distance or peak-season summer moves, book 4–6 weeks in advance to lock in your preferred date.
Are you licensed and insured?
+
Yes. DP Movers Group LLC is fully USDOT licensed and carries full cargo and liability insurance on every move.
How is my quote calculated?
+
Quotes are based on inventory size, distance, access, and services required. We provide written, binding estimates — no surprise charges on moving day.
Do you provide packing supplies?
+
Yes. We provide all packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, dish-pack inserts, wardrobe boxes, and custom crating for specialty items.
What if something gets damaged?
+
All moves include full-value protection. We resolve it directly — no third-party claims process.
Do you charge for stairs or long carries?
+
Any applicable fees are discussed and agreed upon before your move date. No surprises.
